Get to Know Janine

“

I am an experienced romance ghostwriter who have been in the field for more than five years. As a person who is very passionate about writing, it is my goal to produce quality-stories for both my clients and their readers. I have worked with various clients in the past but I always make sure to present an original concept and story pitch for every new project.

Contemporary Romance

I write contemporary romance that blends real-world emotions with unforgettable love stories.

Mafia Romance/Thriller Romance

I love weaving intense Mafia romance and thrilling, high-stakes love stories that keep readers on the edge of their seat.

Fantasy (Shifter, Werewolf, Vampire, etc.)

I also specialize in making fantasy romance stories where passion meets otherworldly danger.

Young Adult Romance

I also don’t shy away in writing exciting young adult romance stories.
